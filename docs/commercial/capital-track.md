# Capital track

The capital track holds the project's funding sources. Each source
has a different legal form; mixing them is not allowed.

## Funding mechanisms

| Source | Form | What the source receives |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Sponsors** | Sponsorship payment (no financial return). | Public or private acknowledgement under the project's sponsorship policy. No equity, no royalties, no profit share. |
| **Strategic customers** | Commercial-track engagement (NRE, licence, support contract). | Deliverables defined under the commercial-track contract. No equity. |
| **External investors** | Separate written investment agreement under the applicable law. | Equity, convertible note, SAFE, or similar instrument as defined in the agreement. Investors do **not** receive automatic rights to project deliverables outside the investment agreement. |
| **ASIC SPV / project financing** | Special-purpose vehicle or project-finance structure for a specific tape-out or board project, future. | Defined per SPV; not in force today. |

## Hard separation

- Sponsorship is **not** investment. A sponsor receives recognition,
  not securities.
- A commercial-track customer is **not** an investor. Paying for a
  contract is paying for delivery, not a stake.
- A contributor is **not** by virtue of contributing entitled to any
  capital-track outcome. See
  [contribution rules (draft)](../onboarding/contribution-rules.md)
  and
  [contributor-vs-sponsor-vs-investor.md](contributor-vs-sponsor-vs-investor.md).

## Disclosure and reporting

Any binding capital-track arrangement is disclosed in the agreement
itself, under the disclosure rules of the applicable law. This
repository does not host the agreements. This page describes the
intended structure, not the actual instruments.

## Status

No external investment agreements are in force as of this page. No
SPV exists for ASIC or project financing. The project's primary
entity, governance, and capital structure live outside this
repository; this page exists to describe the intended track
separation, not to constitute it.

## Solicitation

This page is **not** a solicitation of funds, an offer of securities,
or investment advice. Any party considering investment in the
project should request the live agreement template through the
project's commercial entity and consult their own legal and
financial advisers.
