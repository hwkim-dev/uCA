# Trademarks

The project claims common-law rights in the marks below through
ongoing public use. Formal registration is TBD; the marks listed
here are claims of use, not statements of registration.

For the canonical entry point and the live filing docket, see
[`TRADEMARKS.md`](../../TRADEMARKS.md) (root) and
[`trademark-filing-log.md`](trademark-filing-log.md) (this directory).
Use `PCCX™` on first prominent mention; do not use registered-mark symbols until
registration is granted in the relevant jurisdiction.

## Common-law claim

These marks are used in commerce in connection with the project's
documentation, repositories, and offerings. The project asserts
common-law trademark rights in the relevant jurisdictions based on
that use. The TM symbol may be used informally; the ® symbol must
not be used until the corresponding mark is actually registered in
the relevant jurisdiction.

## Registration plan (TBD)

Formal trademark registration is TBD pending legal review of:

- which jurisdictions require registration first (likely Republic of
  Korea as the project's primary entity location, plus key target
  markets);

  components, computer hardware, software, related services);
- which marks are core (PCCX itself) versus product-line specific
  (ProCore, ASICKit) versus optional defensive filings.

## Use guidelines

Until the formal registration plan is decided, internal and
external use should:

- Use the marks consistently as proper nouns; do not pluralise them
  in normal copy ("the PCCX architecture", not "the PCCXes").
- Reserve the ® symbol for genuinely registered marks in the
  jurisdictions where they are registered. Do not use ® before
  registration.
- For third-party use of the marks, refer to the eventual published
  trademark policy. Until that policy exists, fair use rules of the
  applicable jurisdiction govern.
